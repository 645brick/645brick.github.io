645 the dream

It started with a want to shoot at night, lowestest of low light and not a sacrifice in degrading iso and digital noise from a sensor.

I had an idea to use an off the shelf speed booster/focal reducer to gain a few stops and get back what the eye can see but what most cameras are unable to capture. Not one had a suitable design for the lenses of old.

The 6x4.5 the 6x6 the 6x7 the greats of the film age.

What full frame strives to be.

Medium format

The ideal size for digital.

The issue arose that whilst great lenses all that is avalible is adapters. so you get an insane crop value if used with the mirror system cameras of today.

Back focal lengths that are more than enough for the frame sizes that medium format held dear.
